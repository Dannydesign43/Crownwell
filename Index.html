Testing

What happens before a batch is approved

A condensed view of the verification path a batch moves through before it is eligible to be sold.

1

Incoming Sample Review

A sample from each incoming batch is logged and set aside before any of it reaches inventory.

2

Independent Lab Analysis

The sample is sent to a third-party laboratory for identity and purity analysis, separate from the manufacturer.

3

Certificate of Analysis

Results are compiled into a certificate of analysis and attached to that specific lot number.

4

Listing Approval

Only batches that clear the purity threshold and documentation review are approved to list.

FAQ

Common questions before you visit the catalog

Who can purchase from Crownwell Research?

Access is restricted to individuals 21 years of age or older who can confirm a legitimate research purpose. The full catalog and pricing are only visible on the main website after that confirmation.

Are these products approved for human or animal use?

No. Every compound is sold strictly for laboratory research use only. Nothing on this page or the main site is intended for human consumption, veterinary use, diagnosis, treatment, cure, or prevention of any condition.

How is purity verified?

Each batch is sampled and sent to an independent laboratory for identity and purity analysis. The results are published as a certificate of analysis tied to that batch's lot number, viewable on the main website.

Where can I find a certificate of analysis for a specific batch?

Certificates of analysis are organized by lot number in the COA library on the main Crownwell Research website, linked in the footer of this page.

Does this page represent the full product catalog?

No. This page exists to explain the compliance, quality, and testing standards behind the catalog. Product listings, pricing, and checkout are only available on the main website.

How do I contact Crownwell Research directly?

Contact details and a support form are available on the main website's contact page, linked below in the footer.
